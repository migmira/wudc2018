FAQS about the Tournament

Will the participants have Wi-Fi access during the tournament?
Yes, details will be released at the beginning of the tournament.

When do rounds start?
All debating rounds start on December 29 in the morning.

When does the Masters competition start?
Masters starts on December 28

When does public speaking competition start?
Public speaking starts on December 28

Am I allowed to buy a judge slot?
You can buy a judge slot from another institution. However, this means you must judge for that institution. This means that you must be in contact with the representative from that institution in order to be able to fill in individual registration. Also, our registration team must be notified. Please send an email to: convenors@wudc2018.mx. We strongly prefer, however, that you buy judge slots from us. Contact us at: convenors@wudc2018.mx

Am I allowed to sell a team slot?
No one is allowed to sell a team slot, therefore, no one is allowed to buy one.

FAQS about Registration

How much is the registration fee for a debater/judge/observer?
Debater / Judge : 520.00 USD
Observer: 1,020.00 USD

How can I register to be an independent adjudicator?
The application consists of a Google Form , and should take 30-45 minutes to complete. The form comprises four short sections: personal information, judging experience, debating experience and a 10-15 minute multiple-choice test. Applicants should follow the instructions on the form, and reach out to adjcoremex18@gmail.com with any questions.

Will there be transportation from the hotel to where the rounds are taking place?
Yes, the registration fee includes this service.

Will transfers be arranged?
All transfers to debating venues and any other activities included in the agenda of the tournament are included. Note that if you miss a transfer bus or, for whatever reason, would like to leave early or arrive later, you must arrange your own transportation. Uber is a great way to get around Mexico City.

Will transportation be provided from the airport to the hotel?
Transfers from the airport to the hotel will be arranged by the Org Comm. If you arrive on December 27th, we will have a team of volunteers waiting for you at the Mexico City airport and we will give you a metrocard. This metrocard will allow you to use the Metrobus which will take you directly to the hotel.

Will transportation be provided from the hotel to the airport?
We will give each participant a metrocard to take the same transfer from the hotel to the airport. This will be given to you in your welcome kit. Should you choose to use that metrocard elsewhere to explore the city you can do so, just remember we will not be giving you an extra metrocard to get back to the airport.

How does rooming work?
We will room participants based on preferences given to us in the individual registration format. We will give participants a time slot to answer with necessary details. Failure to do so will mean we may not be able to accomodate you. If you wish to share a room with someone, the person/people you indicate you want to share a room with must also indicate they want to share a room with you in order for us to accomodate that preference.

Someone is coming with me and they have not paid registration fee, can they stay at the hotel?
No, they may not stay at the hotel. If you have an extra guest and they want to be part of the program, they must pay a fee. Please email: convenors@wudc2018.mx

Can I have a single room?
Only if you pay the difference. If you would like this email: convenors@wudc2018.mx
