FAQS about Registration

How much is the registration fee for a debater/judge/observer?
Debater / Judge : 520.00 USD
Observer: 1,020.00 USD

How can I register to be an independent adjudicator?
The application consists of a Google Form , and should take 30-45 minutes to complete. The form comprises four short sections: personal information, judging experience, debating experience and a 10-15 minute multiple-choice test. Applicants should follow the instructions on the form, and reach out to adjcoremex18@gmail.com with any questions.

To whom may I speak if I need a payment extension?
You should send an email explaining your situation in detail to finance@wudc2018.mx or contact directly your contact person Rodolfo: rodolfo.flores@wudc2018.mx , Carlos: carlos.cota@wudc2018.mx or Enrique: enrique.torres@wudc2018.mx

What if I need an invoice?
Participants and Institutions who wish WUDC to request an invoice for Registration Fees need to fill out a form
Form of Request

Estimated time of reply amounts to 48 hours after the request has been submitted. Please consider that depending on the volume of invoices requested, the estimated time of reply could potentially increase to one week (Participants and Institutions under this situation will be notified in advance).
If you have any questions or in case your invoice has not been submitted in a timely manner please contact us directly at finance@wudc2018.mx
Notes:
1) Invoices will be issued in USD only. 2) For illustrative purposes, to translate invoice amount in USD to Mexican pesos please consider the FX published by Mexico´s Central Bank as of the payment date. For details on how to retrieve the above mentioned FX details and on payment verification protocols please refer to the "Payment Verification Protocol"
