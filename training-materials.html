Debating & Judging Manual

For those of you that want to prepare well in advance —either as debaters or judges— for WUDC we offer you the following manual published by the Dutch WUDC 2017 Adjudication Core (and the combined result of many other contributors). We believe that the manual will be particularly helpful for new debaters or judges; however, it should also serve as valuable resource for more experienced debaters and judges to brush up on certain points they feel unsure about.

The WUDC México 2018 Adjudication Core will release an updated version of the manual in November 2018.
