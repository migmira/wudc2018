Updated Version of 1st List of Payments

Dear participants,

Please find attached the list of payments and registration status updated as of May 17th 2017 at 11:59pm. Please read through it carefully and follow these steps to fully confirm your registration for round 1:

  • Step 1: Identify your institution on the list.
  • Step 2: Verify your allocated slots for round 1 and check that the payments due for your institution match
  • Note 1 - If your institution does not show or the allocated slots are incorrect please contact registration@wudc2018.mx with cc to finance@wudc2018.mx
  • Step 4: Check if we have received your proof of payment.
  • Step 5: Check your registration status
  • Note 2 - If you have not provided a proof of payment you need send your documentation and confirm verification with the finance team in a lapse no longer than 72 hourss. After this time period without proof your allocations for round one will be dropped.
  • Note 3 - If your status is either "Potential drop in 72 hours" or "Incomplete supporting documentation - send ASAP to avoid potential drop out". Please contact finance@wudc2018.mx ASAP. After 72 hours without receiving your institutions pending documentation your allocations for round one will be dropped.

    • Note, that even if your status is "OK" this does not mean your payments have been fully tracked in our accounts or that you have not under- or overpaid your registration fees.

    Please expect the finance team to reach out during the course of this week to wrap up final details of your upcoming payments and to track your deposits for the publication of the detailed list of outstanding balances on May 21st 2017

    If you have any questions do not hesitate to reach out to us.
