Final List of Payments for the First Round of Allocations

Dear participants,

The final payment list for the first round of payments has been closed and you may find it attached to this section.

Please consider we have been swamped by emails during these days. If we have missed to correctly show your status on the list please contact finance@wudc2018.mx cc’ing registration@wudc2018.mx. We urge debating clubs to please do this ASAP to correct their allocations.

Please remember that institutions missing supporting documentation have until Friday 26th May to provide it and avoid being dropped. We will publish another list we need you to review as well on Saturday 27th May.

Finally, for institutions that will be dropped for the second round of allocations: please consider that you will be automatically placed at the bottom of the waiting list. Hence you could potentially be re-allocated with slots on the future so please keep in touch and closely follow our communications in case new slot allocation opportunities arise.

Also, please consider you can register again and maybe during the year you can secure more funds to attend WUDC 2018 and continue your registration process. For the time being, however, we have to give room for the teams on the waiting list who have been patiently waiting and asking about possible allocation opportunities.

If you have any questions please feel free to contact us directly,

Regards,

WUDC México 2018 Finance Department.
